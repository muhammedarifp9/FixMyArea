Our Vision & Mission

Empowering the citizens of Kozhikode to build a better, cleaner, and safer city through radical transparency.

Our Vision

To create a deeply connected civic framework where no public issue goes unnoticed. We envision a Kozhikode where citizens and authorities work hand-in-hand through trust and accountability to modernize our beautiful city.

Our Mission

To provide an accessible, user-friendly digital platform that bridges the gap between public infrastructure failures and municipal response times. We demand proof of resolution, not just empty promises.

About FixMyArea

FixMyArea was born out of a simple frustration: seeing the same potholes, broken streetlights, and overflowing waste bins ignored for months. We built this platform for the people. By utilizing crowd-sourced reporting and a transparent verification system, we ensure that the most critical issues in Kozhikode are highlighted, prioritized, and eventually resolved with photographic proof.
